The 'Bad Touch'

Unlike the good touch, the bad touch is a touch that makes you feel...not so good. It makes you feel uncomfortable or scared.
For example, if a stranger touched you on your chest, or the part between your legs, you'd feel uncomfortable, right? That's a bad touch.
But whether the person touching you wants to make you uncomfortable or not, remember- you should always tell an adult.

hola, humans

Normally, a bad touch happens in sensitive areas, like the areas covered by your bathing suit. Some places are okay to touch, like your hands. Other places you'll need permission to touch- like arms and legs. Then, there are places which cannot be touched at all, which are the parts hidden by your bathing suit, your mouth, or any other private parts.
