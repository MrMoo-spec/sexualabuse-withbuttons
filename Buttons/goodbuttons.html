The 'Good Touch'

The GOOD TOUCH is a touch that makes one feel good, or has no impact on one's emotions.
Basically, a good touch is a touch you have no problem with- like a high-five from a friend, a hug from your parents,
or a handshake from a teacher. However, this doesn't mean that you don't feel uncomfortable by doing this. As long as you are okay with it, it's fine
